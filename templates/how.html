How our whistleblowing service works

Service Commissioners and Regulators all expect the highest standards of behaviour of those who work for your organisation, employees see, hear and experience difficult issues within the workplace and often feel frightened to raise their concerns. Early knowledge and involvement allows organisations to deal with any situation confidently and effectively.

By signing up for our service you will join the hundreds of organisations who are protected by Carewhistle.

What we provide

  • Free onboarding and set-up
  • Free branded promotional posters with your company logo
  • Unlimited Report Recipients
  • Anonymous Password Protected Disclosure Process
  • Confidential reporting facility available 24/7/365
  • Simple online reporting portal using end to end ISO 27001 secure encrypted servers
  • Fully trained advisors
  • Independent, impartial and non-judgemental
  • Strict Data Protection standards
  • Free Whistleblowing & Complaints Handling Toolkit
  • Ongoing updates on legislation
  • Full relationship management

Once a report is received, our trained advisors will liaise with the report maker and compile a detailed concern report and email this directly to the appointed coordinator(s) within your organisation… the report maker will be issued with a unique password allowing them to have a confidential line of communication with us at all times.

Once you have completed your investigation… we will provide the employee with an update on any outcome and details of what action has been taken.

Who do we help?

  • CQC registered residential and nursing homes
  • Supported living & domiciliary care
  • Day Services and respite providers
  • Ofsted registered children’s homes and nurseries
  • Non-regulated adult/young people services
  • GP surgeries and Dental practices

Why Carewhistle?

Our service is a cost-effective way to demonstrate that you take raising concerns seriously… Staff can be assured their identity is protected at all times and regulators can be assured you are against closed cultures.
